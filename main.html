I am working as a researcher in Living Analytics Research Centre at School of Computing and Information Systems, Singapore Management University under supervision of Prof. Archan Misra. My research is primarily focussed in the areas of the Embedded Sensing, Low-power System Design, and Internet-of-Things (IoT).

Prior to this, I have worked as a visting researcher with Prof. Shwetak Patel (University of Washington, Seattle, USA) and Sidhant Gupta (Microsoft Research, Redmond) at Ubicomp Lab at Paul G. Allen Centre for Computer Science at University of Washington, Seattle, WA (USA). Post completion of my Ph.D., I have also spent sometime consulting with few technology startups like Aerogram India Pvt. Ltd. and Child Health Imprints Pte. Ltd.

Before joining IIIT, I have worked with Hewlett-Packard Educational services (Noida) and Indrion Technologies (Bangalore) as Embedded Design Engineer in WSN for Smart Energy applications. I did B.Tech (Hons.) ECE in 2011 from Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak.
